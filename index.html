Help California keep the lights on by

playing your part during a Flex Alert.

What is a
Flex Alert?

A Flex Alert is an appeal for

Californians to reduce their energy

usage during heat waves to

maintain a stable power grid.

Why is it
important?

Flex Alerts help California save

energy and are typically issued

during the summer, when

electricity use spikes. Know when

a Flex Alert is issued by signing

up for texts and email.

How it works:

1. Sign up!

For Power Saver Rewards.

2. Save!

Energy during a Flex Alert.

3. Help!

And do your part.

4. Get Credit!

On your energy bill.

Who is your utility provider? Click through to learn more and sign up for Power Saver Rewards.

Cost rewards earned through energy conservation actions during a Flex Alert event will be credited to a future bill statement. To find more details on how cost rewards are calculated and applied to your bill, and additional program details, visit your local utility provider’s Power Saver Rewards website.

Flex Alerts

Why

Flex Alerts

happen.

STEP 1

Heat waves hit California every

summer. And when it’s extra hot,

we use more electricity to help us

stay cool.

STEP 2

But all that energy use puts a

strain on our energy sources and

requires us to be more efficient.

STEP 3

The good news is together we can

save energy and keep California

powered up. All you have to do is sign

up for Flex Alerts to stay in the know.

Check out these tips to

Before 4 P.M.

PRE-COOL

Run your AC cooler

during the day to enjoy

a cool evening

CHORES

Use major appliances

early and enjoy your

evening worry-free.

WINDOW SHADES

Enjoy the sun in the A.M.

and adjust window

coverings in the P.M. to

keep your home cool.

Between 4-9 P.M.

THERMOSTAT

Take a load off your AC

and set your thermostat

to 78 degrees or higher.

DELAY

Take a load off your

appliances and delay

dishes and laundry until

the morning.

LIGHTS OUT

Take a load off your

lights and turn off what

you don’t need. Make it

a game – it can be fun!
